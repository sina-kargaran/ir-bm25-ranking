BM25 Parameters

k1: 1.2 (term frequency saturation parameter)

BM25 Formula

$$\text{BM25}(D, Q) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \log \frac{M + 1}{df(q_i)} \cdot \frac{f(q_i, D) \cdot (k_1 + 1)}{f(q_i, D) + k_1 \cdot \left(1 - b + b \cdot \frac{|D|}{\text{avgdl}}\right)}$$
